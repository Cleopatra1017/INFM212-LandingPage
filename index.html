The Websites I Like

  • 1:MIMO

    -I am still pretty new to the world of web development so I use MIMO to help me practice. In addition, to it being a very well made website, the method of teaching that they utilize is through gaming and this makes it very unique and helpful. So I definitley recommend it to anybody that is looking for a creative and interactive way to practice programing.

  • 2:Chipotle

    -As most of you may already know, Chipotle is a restaurant. Personally, it is one of my very favorites. Especially in regards to the website, everything is so easily accessible, and it features a nutrition calculator, rewards, catering, and a few other essentials.

  • 3:ZARA

    -I've always viewed ZARA as one of those clothing lines that play a perfect intermediate role between luxury and affordable fashion. Their clothing is nothing short of exemplary, and the same goes for their website. Their site has a simple yet classic tone that delivers a great experience for the user.

Additionally, I noticed that some of their flying vehicles had wires attached to them at the top to help them float. Lastly, I must admit the technology sold at Arngren reminded me of the movie Back To The Future because of all the "futuristic" gadgets and gizmos that they claim to have for sale. Please press the link to see for yourself.
